2. All about Chips
==============================================================

1. Class
Each chip is part of a large group of chips.  In BN6, there 
are 3 types
folder. There are:

200 Standard
45 Mega
5 Giga
17 Secret (15 Navi chips from other version of game + 2 standard chips)

per game.

2. Code
Each chip has a letter code assigned to it.  Codes are very important.  
You can select 2 or more chips of the same code to use together.

3. Type
Each chip has a certain type to it.  The type can be considered like an
element.  There are many different types of elements in BN6.

They are:

Fire, Aqua, Electric, Wood, Sword, Wind, Breaking, Targetting,
Number, and Obstacle.

4.  Elemental Wheel
It's important to understand that some types of attacks are
like in Pokemon, super effective, dealing 2x damage against 
certain types of enemies.  The wheel goes.

Fire > Wood > Elec > Aqua > Fire

and

Sword > Wind > Targetting > Breaking > Sword.

5.  Megabyte System
Chip megabytes are an important aspect for tagging, presetting,
and number of chips you can put in folder.

A. You cannot preset chips higher than your regular memory.
B. You cannot tag chips with a combined MB more than 60.
C. # of a particular chip you can put into folder.
   a. 0-19 MB - 5
   b. 20-29 MB - 4
   c. 30-39 MB - 3
   d. 40-49 MB - 2
   e. 50+ MB - 1

Codes: E / Stars:**** / Damage: 140-240
Element: Depends / MB: 66
Flinching? Some yes, some no.

different colors.  Order is Red, Blue, Yellow, Green for
Fire, Aqua, Electric, Grass.  Press A to stop him.  He'll
use a different attack depending on which element you choose.

1. If Fire, he'll use one meteor on every enemy (flinches).  

2. In Aqua, he freezes in widesword range the enemies and the panels 
in front of him (if he freezes an enemy, the panel below him won't 
turn to ice, non-flinching).  

3. In Elec, he'll lightning bolt the 3 panels 3 columns in front 
of him regardless of his position (flinching)  Those panels get broken.  

4. In Wood, he'll turn the entire field into Grass and use 
wood tower that hits the row he's on (non-flinching).
Range:
Fire
 It will automatically hit the enemy.
